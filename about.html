About Me & Contact Info


My name is Faye. My passions and interests include reading and writing; film; music; horror; comics and manga; and professional wrestling.

I used to blog on a relatively consistent basis back in 2010 to 2012. I've recently found myself itching to seriously get back into writing in general. Thus, I've created this new blog to serve as a space for me to write about a variety of topics. My current plans for articles mainly consist of reviews of some of the notable manga and comics that I read last year - but I'd ultimately love to write about lots of different things as time goes on.

I am currently learning web development through The Odin Project and its curriculum. I figured why not use the skills I've picked up thus far to create my own blog/website from scratch?
